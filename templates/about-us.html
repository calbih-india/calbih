Advanced Analytics Units for Public Finance (AAUPF) has been established at Centre for Economic Policy and Public Finance (CEPPF) to develop analytics applications on Public Finance and socio economic indicators of Bihar.

The emphasis of AAUPF is to create cloud-based analytics applications and data management platforms to organize public finance information of the State. In addition, AAUPF would also digitalize the Economic Survey of the State and create GIS enabled applications to correlate data and garner insights for effective policy formulation.

AAUPF is supported by the Department of Finance, Government of Bihar and is hosted at the Asian Development Research Institute, Patna, Bihar.
