What We Do

  • Home
  • What We Do

Our work at Advanced Analytics Unit for Public Finance (AAUPF) focuses on creation of data and information management applications on public finance and socio-economic indicators and preparation of guidelines for effective data governance. Some of our work include:

 

  • Development of Analytics Reporting Tools: 
    Analytical Reporting is critical for effective policy analysis, assessment, and formulation. AAUPF is developing geo-spatial and graphic visualisation centred analytics applications on various socio-economic indicators and public finance data of Government of Bihar.
  • Creation of Integrated Database Management System (IDMS): 
    IDMS platform would help in integration and maintenance of wide range of socio-economic data of the State. Since a large set of data is sourced from different departments making it increasingly difficult to maintain and analyse, cloud-based IDMS would help in integration, quality evaluation, and analysis of primary and secondary level government data. It would also facilitate design of data governance protocols for different departments to further improve data quality and reduce data redundancy in data to be used in economic survey.
  • Development of Data Governance Policy:
    AAUPF would advise Government of Bihar on Data Governance Policy. This would help in improving the entry, normalization, maintenance, and regulation of data across departments. The idea is to create department wise data governance protocols to help institutions effectively access data for policy formulation and policy analysis.
  • Establishment of Bihar Public Finance Data Bank: 
    The Centre for Economic Policy and Public Finance (CEPPF) and Government of Bihar engages with diverse group of international and national institutions for academic research and studies on different socio-economic indicators of the State.
    The foundation of these academic works is the primary and secondary socio-economic data of the State. AAUPF plans to establish a subscription-based online data bank and data exchange portal to improve data access for academic work and policy analysis.
